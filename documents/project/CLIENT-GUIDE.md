# Glossary of Terms

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Account Owner** | The person who registered the account (e.g., Maria) |
| **Patient** | The person receiving medical care (could be account owner or their dependent) |
| **Dependent** | Someone booked under another person's account (e.g., child, spouse, elderly parent) |
| **Queue Number** | Unique identifier for position in line (e.g., PED-004) |
| **Triage** | Initial assessment by nurse, recording vital signs |
| **Medical Record** | Complete documentation of one clinic visit |
| **Consultation Type** | Category of medical service (Pediatrics, OB, General Medicine) |
| **Hospital Drug** | Medication sold by the clinic (appears in bill) |
| **External Drug** | Medication patient must buy elsewhere (not in bill) |
| **Professional Fee** | Doctor's consultation charge |
